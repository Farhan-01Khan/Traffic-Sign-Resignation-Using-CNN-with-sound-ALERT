Our Project

For our final year project, we have successfully developed a machine learning model using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and LeNet-5 architecture. This model is designed to detect 43 different traffic signs on the road. One of the unique features of our model is its ability to detect traffic signs in real-time through a laptop camera, as well as from images uploaded from a local drive. Our model boasts an impressive accuracy rate of 99%, demonstrating its reliability and effectiveness. The development of this model not only showcases our technical skills and understanding of machine learning concepts but also our commitment to creating practical and impactful solutions.

Our Achievements
